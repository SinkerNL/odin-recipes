Steak Dinner in Airfryer


Description

We will create an easy to make porkchop prepared in the airfryer. A simple recipe which only takes about 5 minutes to prepare and just 20 minutes cooking time.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pork chops
  • 2 garlic
  • Salt/pepper
  • Tea spoon of paprika powder
  • Tea spoon of basilicum
  • Spoon of oil

Steps

  1. Cut and clean garlic
  2. Combine all ingredients except for the pork in a bowl
  3. Massage the pork with the mixture
  4. Warm the Airfryer up at 180 degrees
  5. Put in for 20 minutes, and turn around after 10 minutes
